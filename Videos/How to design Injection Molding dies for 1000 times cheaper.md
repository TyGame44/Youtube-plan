# How to design Injection Molding dies for 1000 times cheaper



We can 3d print resin that is thermally resistant and durable enough for injection molding.



This brings the cost of making an injection molded plastic part to just a few pennies to a dollar or two for the mold.



Imagine the hundreds of thousands of dollars this will save when designing and prototyping new plastic injection parts.



It also has huge potential in smaller batch production where plastic injection would typically be too expensive.
