# How to actually make electric trucks



Supercapacitors and no-stop charging stations on highways.



We would have a hybrid design with traditional batteries for the beginning pickup and the end dropoff parts of the trip.



The goal would be to only use the energy capacity in the batteries for a small amount of the trip.



Almost all the energy use by the trucks should be the supercapacitors that are periodically charged along the highways.



This makes electric trucks much easier to manufacture and much more efficient. Plus there would be much less downtime for charging.



Overall it addresses almost all of the problems preventing electric trucks  from coming onto the market.
