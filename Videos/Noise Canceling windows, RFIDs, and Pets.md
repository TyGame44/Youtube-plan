# Noise Canceling windows, RFIDs, and Pets



I live next to a busy road. I’ve been considering vibrating the windows to produce active noise canceling.



You could build this with a speaker attached to each window with its own microcontroller and battery.



Or what if you could do it entirely through RFID stickers. I remember learning about an artist that used RFID Leds in her fingernails so her nails would light up around scanners.



I experimented with adding RFID Leds to my warhammer models.



So what if you could power an RFID with a beam formed wifi signal or something and produce active noise cancelling with a single sticker and existing wifi routers.



After considering that I also wonder about the RFID tags in pets. Could those be powered and used to play audio? What about recording and relaying audio?



Very interesting stuff.



But that's just a theory. A noise canceling theory. And thanks for watching.
