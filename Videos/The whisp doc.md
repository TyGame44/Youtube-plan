You can make them environmentally friendly by bubbling plasma through the water you used to clean them.


You can make a wisp into a camera.
You have several options. You can turn tree seeds into genetically modified lenses.

The most advanced way to make whisps is to make them into momentary linear accelerators. If they can vibrate and you can sample that fast, it’s even better. 
The key part of this idea is that the vibration/linear acceleration must approach the speed of light so you gain the relativistic headlight effect.


This method requires many samples and positional awareness. Then you can properly reconstruct the image.
