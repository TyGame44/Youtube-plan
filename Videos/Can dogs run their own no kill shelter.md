# Can dogs run their own no kill shelter



I would like to propose a novel method for running a no-kill shelter with minimal costs and maximum quality of life for the dogs.



I would like to see if we can bridge the gap between drones, AI, and dog behavior. Dogs are social animals that respond well to pavlovian training.



We would start with a small number of easily trainable dogs. We would test out methods of autonomous training with drones. The goal will be for dogs to complete farming tasks with drone assistance / guidance.



The hope would be that new dogs would be able to pick up on behavioral cues from experienced dogs.



We could also use profiling to identify what jobs fit the dogs best and maximise their quality of life.



I just think it would be really beautiful to build a dog utopia. Especially in the age of AI, it could act as a symbol of hope for a bright future with us working alongside technology.



I did the math and we only need one acre for every forty dogs.  We would have to consider the ethics of excess value that the dogs create. A certain amount of value needs to be reinvested into the technology and the farming supplies but beyond might not be necessary for a dog utopia.
