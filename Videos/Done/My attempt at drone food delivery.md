# My attempt at drone food delivery



I believe that for drone food delivery to ever take off, that we need large drone “busses” that alternate between circling in an area and traveling between areas.



These “Buses” would be designed as highly efficient gliders. We can already create highly efficient gliders that can run indefinitely off of their solar power. The efficiency gains of fixed wings vs drone propellers is well established.



To lean into the glider efficiency, we can make the glider offset its fixed wing so that circling would be almost as efficient as gliding.



I want to create a drone hub that collects and deploys food delivery quadcopters. The quadcopters would pick up food and fly to a hub that travels to the desired neighborhood.

After a dynamic queuing window, the drone “bus” would glide to its destination and the quadcopters would drop off the food.



This would let us actually scale up drone food delivery without the battery limitations interfering with profitability.
