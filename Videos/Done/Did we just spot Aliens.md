# Did we just spot Aliens?



The central molecular zone (CMZ) is in the center of the milky way. Next to our galactic black hole.



In early 2025, the Alma telescope noticed unique narrow structures called filaments in the CMZ. These structures contain large amounts of Silicone Oxide and organic chemistry. Two things that are uncommon elsewhere in the galaxy.



Let's take a quick physics detour.



The penrose process or superradiance is a process that uses a black hole to generate power. It is one of the most advanced theoretical power generation techniques. The theory describes dropping objects into black holes and extracting the energy released.



In the central molecular zone we have found these filaments. They are a stable (not expanding or contracting) formation of this silicone oxide. And it just so happens that Silicone Oxide makes an excellent lens.



Now for the conspiracy theory.



These silicone structures look like a lens array that is projecting power from our galactic black hole to another galaxy. They have organic chemistry that could be programmed to do maintenance work and they uniquely have the correct material for lenses.



If you were a sufficiently advanced civilization. You would have your self replicating drone swarm acquire the power of as many black holes as possible.



But that's just a theory. A conspiracy theory. And thanks for watching.
