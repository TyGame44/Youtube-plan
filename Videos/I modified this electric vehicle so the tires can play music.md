# I modified this electric vehicle so the tires can play music



I have always loved electronics. When I was young, I built speakers out of plastic cups and wire or I built little robots with motors.



One day, I connected the music output to a speaker to see how the motor would spin. To my surprise, it started playing music.



Later on I learned how motors worked and made the connection between the identical structures inside both devices.







So now as an adult, I want to create a fun demonstration of this idea. I am going to modify this Tesla to play music.







What if we could use this to prank people as well. I want to make it sound like the car farted but I don’t want to play a fart sound for the whole neighborhood.







So let's apply a concept I learned in physics class.



If we play the audio from the four tires at slightly different timings, we can create a constructive and deconstructive interference pattern that lets us beam from this audio in any direction we want and enables us to localize it for just one person.







So let's go take this tesla to the club and show off the music. And on the way we can prank people by farting at them.
