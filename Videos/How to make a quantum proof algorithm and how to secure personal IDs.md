# How to make a quantum proof algorithm and how to secure personal IDs



Let's take inspiration from the Monster group in math and the p=np problem.



We need to make an algorithm that expands in complexity faster than an idealized quantum computer can expand its processing power into 4d space time.



I propose a flexible set of challenging problems that feed their solutions into the choice of the next complex problem.



This should make a combined algorithm that either is too spatially complex for a specialized quantum computer and falls back to the limitations of modern computers with a generalized quantum computer.



If we want a safe personal identity system, we must have durable quantum proof security. In WW2, more jews died in Holland than in France because the Dutch had better records. We don’t want the wrong people to get access to this sensitive personal data so strong security first identity verification is crucial.



We need a system of many key exchanges to verify your accounts with any government id without any ability to connect those two pieces of information. Verification should exist to verify a person is real and singular. And potentially block adult content. But no site should be able to identify who you are with this ID.
