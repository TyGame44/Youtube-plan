# Is the universe really expanding?



For a long time we have identified dark matter and noticed that it looks like the entire universe is expanding away from us.



This could be just an illusion of light climbing to us from gravity wells.



If we are in a particularly sparse part of the universe than light that we see from other galaxies would experience redshifting that would also look like universal expansion.



I am very excited to see new gravity maps of the observable universe and updated measurements of dark matter and expansion that don’t assume the universe is uniform.
