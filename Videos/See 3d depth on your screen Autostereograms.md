# See 3d depth on your screen: Autostereograms



This technology is so cool. It lets you see depth on a 2d screen. To see this illusion you will have to take the two dots and line them up. You will have succeeded if you can see one dot in the middle and 2 faint dots to the sides.



I designed a novel shader approach to autostereograms to render them in real time with potential for a full game designed with realistic 3d depth that you can play on your current monitor.



The algorithm works by raymarching over the depth information to stack the noise into perceivable depth.
