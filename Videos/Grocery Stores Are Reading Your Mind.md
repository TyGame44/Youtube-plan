# Grocery Stores Are Reading Your Mind!



In 2021, a research paper presented SpeeChin, a device to transcribe your inner voice or subvocalization.



Around this time I had also learned to meditate and quiet my inner voice to help with my ADHD.



Then something weird happened. I am a white male that has never experienced being treated like a shoplifter in retail stores.



After I learned to quiet my inner voice, I started to notice employees treating me like a shoplifter in retail stores. They would keep their eyes on me as I was shopping.



But this only happened in the large, retail, chain stores. So I concluded that the SpeeChin algorithm was probably being used to predict shoplifting and preemptively flag potential shoplifters.



I think I noticed this mostly in rite aid, walmart, and bestbuy. But no smaller or local stores.
