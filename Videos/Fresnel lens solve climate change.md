# Fresnel lens solve climate change

As a species, we are only beginning to harness the purest energy source we have, the sun. Over the next century, I expected a radical change in our power infrastructure.


We can make cheap, lightweight, plastic fresnel lenses that we float in the atmosphere. These lenses could redirect sunlight onto power infrastructure. They would also have the capability to revolutionize industrial heating.


Eventually when the technology gets cheap enough. We can use these cheap plastic fresnel lenses to reduce the negative impacts of climate change and reduce the chaos in the weather system by leveraging the photon kick effect on the oceans.


This would let us directly regulate the evaporative cooling of our oceans. The scale required for a non power / fossil fuels impact on climate change is massive but we have scaled up other similar technologies.


This also doesn’t reduce the value of fossil fuels. The dumbest thing you can do with oil is burn it. We can consider that we have found methane in space for short term economics. But the methane / more complex parts of oil are still significantly more valuable as methane / more complex molecules. Rather than as C02. C02 is basic.
