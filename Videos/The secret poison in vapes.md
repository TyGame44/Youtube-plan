# The secret poison in vapes



Vapes should be much healthier than cigarettes. They only have the nicotine and none of the tar and carcinogens.



Despite this, they still seem to cause cancer. Some of this comes from poor manufacturing and other design issues with cheap vapes.



But I propose another element that is often ignored for both smoking and vaping. Even with smoking, we can only account for about 50% of the increased risk of lung cancer.



I propose that the heat is the other 50% risk in cigarettes and might contribute to almost all of the cancer risk associated with vaping.



In India, there is one village that drinks boiling tea. They have much higher throat cancer rates.



I would love to create a mouthpiece that tracks flow and temperature of many different vapes. This could be a collaboration with a university to do health scans. Or we could make these mouthpieces mass produced enough to collect data over a multi year period.



We could also establish a baseline heat for many different brands and compare that with current cancer rates for different smokers.



But there is also another cursed element to cigarettes and cancer rates. Cigarettes in the USA cause almost twice as much cancer vs europe. This was because people would smoke in bed and light their houses on fire. So they doubled the cancer rate by adding flame retardant to all cigarettes.
