# 80% Solar efficiency? Power beamed from space!?!?!
Suncell claims to have a validated system that uses the superradiance of hydrogen to collect and reemit light. By targeting hydrogen super-radiance, they claim to achieve ~80% energy conversion. This is almost double current solar efficiency and could significantly reduce the cost of solar farms.

Rather than many panels, we could have a single collector and a floating plastic fresnel lens that guides all of the light in an area towards the collector.
