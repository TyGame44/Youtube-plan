# Trees are natures Air Conditioner



Trees use less than 1% of their energy for growth.



Most of the energy a tree consumes is to cool itself and its surroundings down.



The majority of this energy goes into evaporative cooling where the tree releases its own water.



Another large amount of energy goes into making the leaves reflective to keep the tree cool.



Trees even have more ways to cool themselves down. They will actually use chemistry to make high energy volatile organic compounds and release them because the chemical reaction will cool down the trees.



This one is crazy. As a last resort, trees start producing VOCs to cool themselves. Isn’t that wild.



So trees spend almost 99% of their energy just to act as air conditioners for themselves and their surroundings.



It now makes much more sense why areas in cities with trees are much cooler than areas without trees. It is not just the shade they provide but a fundamental symbiotic relationship that they have with their environment.
